Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Infinmax Consultant Investments and Wealth Management provide?

We specialize in financial planning, investment portfolio management, retirement planning, tax strategies, and risk management, tailored to meet individual and corporate needs.

How do I start investing with Infinmax Consultant?

To get started, book a consultation with our experts. We’ll analyze your financial goals, risk appetite, and investment preferences to create a personalized plan.

What is the minimum investment required?

The minimum investment amount depends on the service package you choose. Contact us to discuss the options best suited for your financial goals.

How does Infinmax Consultant ensure my investments are secure?

We follow stringent risk management practices, conduct regular market analysis, and diversify portfolios to minimize risk and safeguard your investments.

Can I track my investment performance?

Yes, we provide real-time tracking tools and periodic performance reports to keep you updated on your investments.

What is the difference between financial planning and wealth management?

Financial planning focuses on setting short-term and long-term financial goals, while wealth management involves managing your overall financial portfolio, including investments, taxes, and estate planning.

What support do you provide after setting up my investment plan?

We offer ongoing support, including portfolio reviews, market updates, and strategy adjustments to ensure your investments align with your evolving goals.
